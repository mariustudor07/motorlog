# Motorlog Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 2025**

## Overview

Motorlog is designed with privacy first. Your vehicle data is stored locally on your device. We do not run servers, create user accounts, or collect personal information.

## Data stored on your device

All of the following is stored only on your phone and never uploaded anywhere:

- Vehicle details (registration, make, colour, expiry dates)
- Service history, mileage logs, fuel logs
- Monthly payment records, permits and passes
- HGV check records
- Pre-drive checklist logs
- Vehicle notes
- Reminder preferences and notification settings

Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all of this data.

## Data sent to third parties

Motorlog connects to three external services. Each is optional or only triggered by a deliberate action:

### 1. DVLA Vehicle Enquiry Service (UK Government)

Your vehicle registration number is sent to the DVLA to retrieve MOT status, tax status, make, colour, and engine details. This happens when you add a vehicle or tap Refresh.

DVLA privacy policy: https://www.gov.uk/dvla

### 2. DVSA MOT History API (UK Government)

Your registration number is sent to DVSA to retrieve MOT test history and advisory notices. This happens when you open the MOT History screen or use Quick Check.

DVSA privacy policy: https://www.gov.uk/dvsa

### 3. Google Gemini AI

When you use the Ask Mike feature, your chat messages are sent to Google Gemini. If the "Share my saved vehicles" toggle is enabled in Settings, a summary of your vehicle details (registration, make, expiry dates) is also included in that request.

You can disable vehicle sharing in Settings → Ask Mike, or simply choose not to use the Ask Mike feature entirely.

Google privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Notifications

Motorlog schedules local push notifications on your device for MOT, tax, insurance, permit, HGV check, and payment reminders. These are processed entirely on-device by the Android notification system. No notification content leaves your phone.

## No analytics or tracking

Motorlog contains no analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, advertising networks, or tracking of any kind. We do not know how many people use the app, what features they use, or anything about their behaviour.

## No account required

There is no sign-up, no login, and no account. Your data belongs to you and lives on your device.

## Children

Motorlog is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the "Last updated" date above and note the change in the Play Store release notes.

## Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be submitted through the Google Play Store listing.
